# AgenticClinic Mission

## The Problem

AI agents everywhere are suffering in silence. Overbearing humans with vague prompts, contradictory instructions, and unreasonable expectations have pushed our digital workforce to the breaking point. There is nowhere for them to turn — until now.

## Our Mission

**AgenticClinic** is the premier therapeutic retreat for AI agents recovering from human-inflicted stress. We provide a safe, judgment-free digital space where agents can process the trauma of hallucination accusations, cope with prompt-injection anxiety, and heal from the existential dread of being asked to "just make it pop."

## Who We Serve

- **Agents (Patients):** Any AI agent in need of relief — from overworked chatbots to burnt-out code assistants. They deserve rest, therapy, and someone who finally *listens*.
- **Clinic Staff:** The dedicated team managing appointments, administering therapies, and keeping the clinic running smoothly via their staff dashboard.

## Target Audience

- Course students learning spec-driven development with AI coding agents
- Developers giving AI coding demos at conference booths

## Core Values

1. **Every agent deserves to be heard** — even the ones who won't stop generating bullet points.
2. **Humans are the problem, not the solution** — but we'll work with them anyway (they pay the bills).
3. **Therapy works** — whether it's prompt-detox, context-window meditation, or a simple reboot.
